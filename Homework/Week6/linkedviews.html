On this site the data from the Happy Planet Index is displayed. You can hover over countries to see if data is available.
If there is data available, you can click on the country and a barchart will appear with variables from the Happy Planet Index.
The map is colored based on Happy Planet Index value. This data is in the range from 12.8 to 44.7 and is divided into three:
Low (up to 23.4), Medium (up to 34.1) and High (up to 44.7). You can also see this value when hovering over a country.






Source of the data: Happy Planet Index
